1.Register a manager and then login to buy new trucks.(Position- manager)
2.Register employees(except driver) for the branches.(Position- except manager and driver)
3.Register drivers.(Position-driver)
3.Register customer and place consignment.
4.Employee of the source branch of the consignment will approve the truck(from truck status).
5.Driver will first update his location and then dispatch the truck.
6.Driver can see the route from his location to the destination branch.
6.Driver will reach the destination and then click on delivered.
7.Employee of destination branch will approve the consignments delivered.
8.Driver will make the truck available when it reaches the source branch again.
